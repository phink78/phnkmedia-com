Privacy Policy

This policy explains how PHNK Media & Design handles personal data on phnkmedia.com, including consent and analytics behavior implemented by this site.

1. Who we are

PHNK Media & Design (Oldeberkoop, Friesland, The Netherlands).

Contact: patrick@phnkmedia.com

This policy applies to pages deployed on phnkmedia.com and source-controlled behavior in this repository.

2. What data we process

  • Basic website usage and technical telemetry only after analytics consent is granted.
  • Contact data you choose to share via email links.

3. Consent behavior on this site

  • On first visit, no analytics scripts are loaded until you explicitly accept analytics.
  • Your choice is stored in local browser storage under key phnk_analytics_consent.
  • Stored values are granted or denied.
  • Your consent choice is stored per calendar month and is requested again on the first day of each new month.
  • If you decline, only necessary site functionality remains active and analytics stay disabled.
  • If you accept, analytics services are loaded and may place their own cookies/identifiers.

4. Analytics services used after consent

  • Google Analytics (Google tag / gtag.js)
  • Amplitude Analytics

These services may process IP address, page views, device/browser metadata, and interaction events for performance and traffic insights.

5. Legal basis

Analytics are processed on the basis of your consent. You can refuse consent without losing access to core website content.

6. How to withdraw or change consent

  • Use the Privacy Settings button in the website footer to reopen the consent choices at any time.
  • Consent is also asked again automatically on the first day of each new month.
  • Clear site storage/cookies for phnkmedia.com in your browser, then reload the site.
  • Or contact us and we can guide you through the steps.

7. Data retention and sharing

  • Retention periods are managed by the analytics providers and their account settings.
  • Data may be processed by third-party providers not controlled by this repository, acting as processors or controllers under their own terms.

8. Your rights

If applicable under GDPR, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or object to processing. Contact us at patrick@phnkmedia.com.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when site behavior or legal requirements change.
